NBA 2020-2021 Season Teams ELO Score

The ELO rating system is a method for calculating the relative skill levels of players in zero-sum games. It was first used in chess. For NBA, each team's initial ELO score is 1600, when a team beats another team, its ELO score increases based on factors such as win expectation and opponent's ELO. So higher is stronger.
